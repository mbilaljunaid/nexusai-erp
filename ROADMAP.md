# NexusAIFirst ERP Roadmap

This document outlines the planned development roadmap for NexusAIFirst ERP.

## Vision

To become the leading open-source enterprise resource planning platform, empowering businesses of all sizes with AI-powered automation and comprehensive business management capabilities.

---

## Current Release: v2.0.0

### Completed Features
- 12 Core Modules (CRM, Projects, Finance, HR, Manufacturing, Supply Chain, Sales, Service, Analytics, Compliance, AI Assistant)
- 812 Dynamic Forms
- 40+ Industry Templates
- 12-Language Support
- AI-Powered Features
- Multi-Framework Compliance (GDPR, HIPAA, SOX, ISO9001, PCI-DSS)
- Integration Hub with API Gateway
