MODULE 22: COMPLIANCE, RISK & AUDIT MANAGEMENT

Purpose: Ensure enterprise-wide regulatory compliance, risk identification, mitigation, internal audits, and governance across ERP, Finance, HR, CRM, Projects, Inventory, EPM, BI, AI, and BPM modules.

Important Note for Replit: Before creating any compliance, risk, or audit objects, workflows, or dashboards, Replit must check for existing implementations. Do not duplicate or recreate anything already built. Only create missing elements or enhancements.

22.1 Regulatory Compliance Management

Forms / Screens: Compliance Rule Setup, Regulatory Requirement Catalog, Compliance Checklist, Compliance Dashboard.

Fields: Rule/Requirement ID, Name, Module/Process, Description, Jurisdiction, Frequency, Responsible Owner, Status (Active, Inactive), Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, regulatory references verified, responsible owner assigned, compliance status tracked.

Workflows: New compliance rules trigger notifications, checklist completion tracked, compliance status updated on dashboards, integration with Finance, HR, Projects, and Inventory for automatic enforcement.

22.2 Risk Management

Forms / Screens: Risk Register, Risk Assessment, Risk Mitigation Plan, Risk Scoring Dashboard, Risk Reports.

Fields: Risk ID, Module/Process, Description, Likelihood, Impact, Risk Score, Category (Operational, Financial, Strategic, Compliance, IT), Responsible Owner, Status (Open, Mitigated, Closed), Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Risk scoring validated, mitigation actions assigned, mandatory fields enforced, approvals enforced for critical risks.

Workflows: New risk triggers notifications, mitigation progress tracked, risk scoring updates dashboards, integration with AI for predictive risk analytics.

22.3 Internal Audit Management

Forms / Screens: Audit Planning, Audit Checklist, Audit Findings, Audit Report, Audit Dashboard.

Fields: Audit ID, Module/Process, Auditor, Scheduled Date, Findings, Severity, Status (Planned, In Progress, Closed), Recommendations, Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Audit schedule validated, findings recorded accurately, approvals enforced, severity classification correct.

Workflows: Audit planning triggers notifications, findings escalated based on severity, resolution tracked, dashboards updated in real-time, integration with Compliance and Risk modules.

22.4 Incident & Issue Management

Forms / Screens: Incident Logging, Investigation, Corrective Action, Incident Dashboard.

Fields: Incident ID, Module/Process, Description, Severity, Status (Open, Investigating, Resolved, Closed), Responsible Owner, Action Taken, Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, severity classification accurate, responsible owner assigned, corrective actions validated.

Workflows: Incident creation triggers notifications, investigations assigned automatically, resolution updates dashboards, recurring issues flagged, integration with BPM and Projects modules for corrective measures.

22.5 Policy & Document Management

Forms / Screens: Policy Repository, Policy Approval, Document Tracking, Acknowledgment Tracking, Compliance Dashboard.

Fields: Policy/Document ID, Title, Type, Department, Owner, Version, Status (Draft, Approved, Active, Archived), Effective Date, Review Date, Acknowledgment Status, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, version control maintained, review dates validated, acknowledgments tracked.

Workflows: New or updated policies trigger approval workflow, notifications sent to stakeholders, acknowledgment tracking updated, dashboards reflect policy compliance.

22.6 Reporting & Dashboards

Forms / Screens: Compliance Reports, Risk Reports, Audit Reports, Incident Reports, KPI Dashboards.

Fields: Report ID, Name, Module, Filters (Date Range, Module, Severity, Status), Data Fields (Findings, Risks, Compliance Status, Incidents, Mitigations), Export Options, Scheduled Execution, Audit Fields.

Validations: Data accuracy verified, filters validated, scheduled reports executed correctly, dashboards updated in real-time, permissions enforced.

Workflows: Scheduled report generation triggers notifications, dashboards update automatically, integration with BI and AI modules for advanced analytics and predictive insights.

22.7 Integrations & Automations

Automations: Risk scoring updates dashboards automatically, compliance checklist notifications, audit reminders, incident escalation workflows, recurring risk alerts.

Integrations: ERP, Finance, HR, Projects, Inventory, CRM, BPM, AI/BI modules, External Regulatory Systems, Document Management Platforms.

Key Features: Regulatory compliance management, risk identification and mitigation, internal audit management, incident tracking and resolution, policy and document management, dashboards and analytics, automated notifications and alerts, predictive risk analytics, cross-module integration, audit-ready and compliance integrated.
