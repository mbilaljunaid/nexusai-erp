35.2 Forecasting & Trend Analysis

Forms / Screens: Forecast Input, Trend Dashboard, Scenario Simulation, What-If Analysis.

Validations: Input data verified, forecast outputs validated, scenarios tested for accuracy.

Workflows: Detected anomalies trigger alerts, corrective workflows initiated, dashboards updated, integration with Compliance, Finance, HR, and Inventory modules.

Workflows: AI insights updated in real-time, alerts generated for critical predictions, integration with BI, EPM, Finance, HR, Projects, and Compliance for decision support.
