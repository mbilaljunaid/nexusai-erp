MODULE 30: CUSTOMER RELATIONSHIP MANAGEMENT (CRM)

Purpose: Manage the complete customer lifecycle including lead management, opportunity tracking, sales, marketing campaigns, service requests, customer interactions, and analytics, integrated with ERP, Finance, Projects, HR, Inventory, EPM, BI, AI, BPM, and Compliance modules.

Important Note for Replit: Before creating any CRM objects, workflows, dashboards, or integrations, Replit must check for existing implementations. Do not duplicate or recreate anything already built. Only create missing elements or enhancements.

30.1 Lead Management

Forms / Screens: Lead Capture, Lead Qualification, Lead Assignment, Lead Dashboard.

Fields: Lead ID, Name, Contact Details, Source, Status (New, Contacted, Qualified, Disqualified), Assigned Sales Rep, Opportunity Link, Priority, Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, duplicate leads prevented, status transitions validated, approvals for high-value leads enforced.

Workflows: Lead creation triggers notifications, assignment updates dashboards, integration with Marketing, Projects, and AI for predictive lead scoring.

30.2 Opportunity & Pipeline Management

Forms / Screens: Opportunity Creation/Edit, Opportunity Stages, Pipeline Dashboard, Forecasting.

Fields: Opportunity ID, Name, Customer/Lead, Project/Product, Stage, Expected Revenue, Close Date, Probability %, Owner, Status, Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, stage transitions validated, revenue calculations accurate, duplicate opportunities prevented.

Workflows: Stage updates trigger notifications, dashboards reflect pipeline health, integration with Finance for revenue recognition, AI modules for predictive close probability.

30.3 Account & Contact Management

Forms / Screens: Account Master, Contact Details, Interaction History, Account Dashboard.

Fields: Account ID, Name, Industry, Type (Customer, Partner, Prospect), Contacts (Name, Role, Email, Phone), Status, Assigned Owner, Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, duplicate accounts/contacts prevented, contact details validated.

Workflows: New account creation triggers notifications, interaction history updated automatically, dashboards reflect account activity, integration with Projects, Sales, and Service modules.

30.4 Sales & Quotation Management

Forms / Screens: Quotation Creation, Sales Order, Approval Workflow, Sales Dashboard.

Fields: Quote ID, Opportunity ID, Customer, Products/Services, Quantity, Price, Discounts, Taxes, Total Amount, Status (Draft, Sent, Approved, Won, Lost), Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Pricing validated, taxes calculated correctly, mandatory approvals enforced, duplicate quotes prevented.

Workflows: Quotation submission triggers approval workflow, approved quotes convert to orders, dashboards updated automatically, integration with Finance and Inventory for billing and fulfillment.

30.5 Marketing Campaigns

Forms / Screens: Campaign Creation, Segmentation, Email/SMS/Notification Setup, Campaign Dashboard.

Fields: Campaign ID, Name, Type (Promotional, Awareness, Nurturing), Target Audience, Start/End Dates, Status, Budget, Metrics (Open Rate, Click Rate, Response Rate), Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, audience segmentation validated, compliance rules enforced, approvals required for high-impact campaigns.

Workflows: Campaign execution triggers notifications, responses captured in CRM, dashboards update in real-time, integration with AI for predictive targeting and personalization.

30.6 Customer Service & Support

Forms / Screens: Case Management, Ticket Logging, SLA Setup, Service Dashboard, Knowledge Base Integration.

Fields: Case ID, Customer, Product/Service, Issue Description, Priority, Status (New, In Progress, Resolved, Closed), Assigned Agent, SLA Target, Resolution Notes, Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, SLA calculations validated, case assignment accurate, duplicate tickets prevented.

Workflows: Case creation triggers notifications, SLA breaches trigger escalations, resolution updates dashboards, integration with AI for automated responses and predictive support.

30.7 Reporting & Analytics

Forms / Screens: Sales Reports, Pipeline Reports, Lead Conversion, Campaign Performance, Customer Insights Dashboard.

Fields: Report ID, Name, Module, Filters (Date Range, Sales Rep, Campaign, Product, Status), Metrics (Revenue, Leads, Opportunities, Cases, Customer Satisfaction), Export Options, Schedule, Audit Fields.

Validations: Data integrity verified, filters validated, dashboards render accurately, scheduled execution tested.

Workflows: Reports generated on-demand or scheduled, dashboards update automatically, AI modules provide predictive insights, integration with BI for advanced analytics.

30.8 Integrations & Automations

Automations: Lead assignment, opportunity notifications, SLA alerts, campaign triggers, customer follow-ups, automated responses.

Integrations: ERP/Finance for revenue recognition, Projects for project-related sales, HR for sales commissions, Inventory for product availability, AI/BI modules for predictive analytics, Compliance for audit.

Key Features: End-to-end CRM management, lead & opportunity management, account & contact management, sales & quotation, marketing campaigns, customer service & support, dashboards & analytics, workflow automation, AI-powered insights, cross-module integration, audit-ready and compliance integrated.
