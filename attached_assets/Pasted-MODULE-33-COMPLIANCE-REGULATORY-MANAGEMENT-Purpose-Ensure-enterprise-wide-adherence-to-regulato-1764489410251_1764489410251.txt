MODULE 33: COMPLIANCE & REGULATORY MANAGEMENT

Purpose: Ensure enterprise-wide adherence to regulatory requirements, internal policies, and industry standards across ERP, Finance, HR, CRM, Projects, Inventory, EPM, BI, AI, BPM, and other modules.

Important Note for Replit: Before creating any compliance or regulatory objects, workflows, or dashboards, Replit must check for existing implementations. Do not duplicate or recreate anything already built. Only create missing elements or enhancements.

33.1 Regulatory Compliance Management

Forms / Screens: Regulation Setup, Compliance Checklist, Compliance Dashboard, Audit Trail, Regulatory Reports.

Fields: Regulation ID, Name, Jurisdiction, Module Impacted, Compliance Requirement, Frequency, Status, Responsible Person, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, jurisdiction mapping correct, compliance requirements validated, duplicate regulations prevented.

Workflows: Regulation assignment triggers notifications, compliance checks automated, dashboards update real-time, integration with Finance, HR, Projects, and EPM for regulatory adherence.

33.2 Policy Management

Forms / Screens: Policy Creation/Edit, Policy Distribution, Acknowledgment Tracking, Policy Dashboard.

Fields: Policy ID, Name, Module, Owner, Effective Date, Version, Status (Draft, Active, Retired), Acknowledgment Status, Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, version control maintained, approvals enforced, acknowledgment tracking validated.

Workflows: Policy updates trigger notifications, employee acknowledgment logged, dashboards reflect compliance status, integration with HR and BPM modules.

33.3 Audit & Inspection Management

Forms / Screens: Audit Plan Setup, Audit Execution, Findings & Observations, Corrective Actions, Audit Dashboard.

Fields: Audit ID, Module, Scheduled Date, Auditor, Findings, Severity, Corrective Action Plan, Status (Planned, In Progress, Closed), Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Audit schedule validated, findings accurately recorded, corrective actions tracked, approvals enforced.

Workflows: Audit initiation triggers notifications, findings escalated automatically, corrective actions monitored, dashboards update in real-time, integration with Finance, HR, Projects, and Inventory.

33.4 Risk & Incident Management

Forms / Screens: Risk & Incident Registration, Assessment, Mitigation Plan, Compliance Dashboard.

Fields: Risk/Incident ID, Module, Description, Severity, Likelihood, Impact, Responsible Person, Status (Open, Mitigated, Closed), Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, severity & impact validated, responsible owner assigned, approvals enforced.

Workflows: Risk or incident creation triggers notifications, escalations for critical items, dashboards update in real-time, AI modules provide predictive risk insights.

33.5 Reporting & Analytics

Forms / Screens: Compliance Reports, Audit Reports, Policy Acknowledgment Reports, Regulatory Dashboards.

Fields: Report ID, Name, Module, Filters (Date Range, Department, Status, Regulation), Metrics (Compliance % Achieved, SLA Breaches, Pending Actions), Export Options, Schedule, Audit Fields.

Validations: Data integrity verified, metrics accurate, scheduled execution tested, dashboards render correctly.

Workflows: Reports generated on-demand or scheduled, dashboards update automatically, integration with BI and AI for predictive compliance insights.

33.6 Integrations & Automations

Automations: Policy acknowledgment reminders, audit notifications, compliance alerts, risk escalation triggers, corrective action follow-ups.

Integrations: ERP/Finance/HR/Projects/Inventory for process compliance, AI/BI modules for predictive monitoring, BPM for workflow integration, EPM for regulatory reporting, Finance for tax & statutory compliance.

Key Features: Enterprise-wide compliance management, regulatory tracking, policy management, audits & inspections, risk & incident management, dashboards & reporting, workflow automation, AI-powered predictive compliance, cross-module integration, audit-ready.
