MODULE 19: CRM & CUSTOMER LIFECYCLE MANAGEMENT

Purpose: Manage all customer-related processes including lead management, opportunity tracking, account management, sales pipeline, marketing campaigns, customer service, and analytics across ERP, Finance, HR, Projects, Inventory, EPM, BI, AI, and Compliance modules.

Important Note for Replit: Before creating any CRM or customer lifecycle management objects, workflows, or reports, Replit must check for existing implementations. Do not duplicate or recreate anything already built. Only create missing elements or enhancements.

19.1 Lead Management

Forms / Screens: Lead Capture, Lead Qualification, Lead Assignment, Lead Status Tracking, Lead Dashboard.

Fields: Lead ID, Name, Contact Details, Source, Lead Owner, Industry, Lead Status (New, Contacted, Qualified, Disqualified), Estimated Value, Notes, Approval Status, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, contact information validated, duplicate leads prevented, lead source verified.

Workflows: New lead triggers notifications to assigned owner, lead qualification triggers workflow for follow-up, status changes update dashboards, integration with Marketing, Sales, and AI modules for scoring and prioritization.

19.2 Opportunity & Pipeline Management

Forms / Screens: Opportunity Creation, Opportunity Stage Management, Pipeline Dashboard, Forecasting, Opportunity Reports.

Fields: Opportunity ID, Account/Customer, Opportunity Name, Stage (Prospecting, Qualification, Proposal, Negotiation, Closed Won/Lost), Expected Revenue, Probability %, Close Date, Assigned Sales Rep, Competitors, Notes, Approval Status, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, probability validated, revenue numeric, stage transitions logical, approvals enforced for discounts or special terms.

Workflows: Stage changes trigger notifications and approvals, pipeline updates dashboards in real-time, AI scoring for prioritization, integration with Finance for revenue recognition and invoicing.

19.3 Account & Contact Management

Forms / Screens: Customer Account Creation/Edit, Contact Management, Account Hierarchy, Account Dashboard, Interaction Logs.

Fields: Account ID, Account Name, Industry, Billing/Shipping Address, Primary Contact, Contacts List, Account Status (Active, Inactive), Annual Revenue, Number of Employees, Relationship Owner, Notes, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, account hierarchy validated, contacts verified, duplicate accounts prevented.

Workflows: Account updates trigger notifications, integration with Sales, Projects, Finance, and Support modules, dashboards reflect account activity, audit logs maintained.

19.4 Marketing Campaigns

Forms / Screens: Campaign Creation, Campaign Segmentation, Email/SMS/Push Campaigns, Campaign Analytics Dashboard.

Fields: Campaign ID, Name, Type (Email, SMS, Social, Multi-Channel), Target Audience, Start/End Dates, Budget, Status, Owner, Metrics (Open Rate, CTR, Conversion Rate), Approval Status, Audit Fields.

Validations: Campaign dates valid, target audience selected, budget numeric, mandatory fields enforced, compliance with regulations (GDPR, CAN-SPAM).

Workflows: Campaign creation triggers approval workflow, execution triggers automated notifications, responses update CRM records, dashboards updated in real-time, integration with AI for personalization and scoring.

19.5 Customer Service & Support

Forms / Screens: Case/Incident Management, Ticket Assignment, SLA Tracking, Knowledge Base, Service Dashboard.

Fields: Case ID, Customer, Issue Type, Priority (Low, Medium, High, Critical), Status (Open, In Progress, Resolved, Closed), Assigned Agent, Resolution Notes, SLA Due Date, Approval Status, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, SLA validated, priority aligned with business rules, duplicate cases prevented.

Workflows: Case creation triggers assignment and notifications, SLA alerts sent automatically, resolution updates dashboards, integration with Knowledge Base, Projects, Finance for billing if applicable.

19.6 Sales & Quotation Management

Forms / Screens: Quote Creation, Quote Approval, Quote to Order Conversion, Discount Approval, Sales Dashboard.

Fields: Quote ID, Customer, Items/Services, Quantity, Unit Price, Discount %, Total Amount, Validity Date, Status (Draft, Approved, Sent, Converted), Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, calculations validated, approval required for discounts above threshold, duplication prevented.

Workflows: Quote submission triggers approval workflow, approved quotes sent to customer, conversion triggers sales order creation, dashboards updated in real-time.

19.7 Analytics & Reporting

Forms / Screens: Sales Reports, Pipeline Reports, Lead Conversion Reports, Customer Activity Reports, CRM Dashboard.

Fields: Report ID, Name, Module, Filters (Customer, Lead, Opportunity, Date Range, Status), Data Fields (Leads, Opportunities, Revenue, Conversion Rate, Customer Activity), Export Options, Scheduled Execution, Audit Fields.

Validations: Data accuracy verified, filters validated, scheduled reports executed correctly, dashboards updated in real-time, permissions enforced.

Workflows: Scheduled report generation triggers notifications, dashboards update automatically, AI-driven insights for sales and marketing, integration with BI and Finance modules.

19.8 Integrations & Automations

Automations: Lead scoring and routing, opportunity stage notifications, SLA alerts for support cases, quote approval routing, marketing campaign automation, AI-driven recommendations.

Integrations: ERP/Finance for invoicing and revenue recognition, Projects for customer-related projects, HR for support staffing, Inventory for product availability, Marketing Automation platforms, AI/BI for analytics and predictive insights.

Key Features: Lead-to-cash process management, account and contact management, opportunity and pipeline tracking, marketing automation, customer service management, quoting and sales order integration, dashboards and analytics, AI-powered insights, cross-module integration, audit-ready and compliance integrated.
