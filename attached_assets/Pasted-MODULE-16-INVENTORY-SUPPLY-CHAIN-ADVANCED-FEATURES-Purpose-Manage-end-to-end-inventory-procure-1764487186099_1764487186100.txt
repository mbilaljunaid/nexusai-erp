MODULE 16: INVENTORY & SUPPLY CHAIN ADVANCED FEATURES

Purpose: Manage end-to-end inventory, procurement, warehouse operations, logistics, demand planning, supplier management, and supply chain optimization across ERP, Finance, Projects, CRM, HR, EPM, BI, AI, and Compliance modules.

Important Note for Replit: Before creating any inventory or supply chain objects, workflows, or reports, Replit must check for existing implementations. Do not duplicate or recreate anything already built. Only create missing elements or enhancements.

16.1 Inventory Master & Item Management

Forms / Screens: Item Creation, Item Edit, Item Categorization, Item Attributes, Item Lifecycle Management, Inventory Dashboard.

Fields: Item ID, Item Name, SKU, Category, Subcategory, Unit of Measure, Standard Cost, Selling Price, Supplier, Storage Location, Reorder Level, Safety Stock, Lead Time, Status (Active, Inactive, Discontinued), Batch/Lot Tracking, Serial Number Tracking, Expiry Date, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, SKU unique, cost and price numeric, supplier assigned, status validated, batch/serial tracking enabled if required.

Workflows: New item triggers approval workflow, updates trigger notifications to warehouse and procurement teams, lifecycle changes reflected in reports and dashboards, integration with Finance and Sales modules for costing and pricing.

16.2 Warehouse & Stock Management

Forms / Screens: Warehouse Setup, Bin & Location Configuration, Stock Inward/Outbound, Stock Transfer, Stock Adjustment, Stock Counting, Inventory Valuation, Inventory Dashboard.

Fields: Warehouse ID, Location, Bin, Item ID, Quantity, Unit of Measure, Batch/Lot, Serial Number, Movement Type (Inward, Outward, Transfer), Cost, Status, Audit Fields.

Validations: Stock transactions validated against available stock, bin/location capacity enforced, cost calculations validated, mandatory fields enforced, serial/batch numbers verified.

Workflows: Stock receipts trigger notifications, stock transfers update multiple warehouses automatically, stock adjustments trigger approvals, inventory reports updated in real-time, integration with Procurement, Sales, and Finance modules.

16.3 Procurement & Supplier Management

Forms / Screens: Supplier Master, Purchase Request, Purchase Order, Supplier Evaluation, Contract Management, Supplier Dashboard.

Fields: Supplier ID, Supplier Name, Contact Details, Supplier Category, Rating, Contract Terms, Lead Time, Payment Terms, Status, Purchase Requests (PR), Purchase Orders (PO), Approval Status, Audit Fields.

Validations: Supplier details complete, mandatory fields enforced, contracts validated, approval workflow enforced for POs above threshold, supplier rating updated periodically.

Workflows: PR triggers approval workflow, PO creation triggers notifications to supplier and procurement team, supplier evaluation updates dashboards, integration with Finance for payment processing.

16.4 Demand Planning & Forecasting

Forms / Screens: Demand Forecast Setup, Forecast Execution, Historical Analysis, Inventory Planning Dashboard, Scenario Planning.

Fields: Forecast ID, Item ID, Forecast Method (Historical, Trend, Seasonal, AI-Powered), Forecast Period, Predicted Demand, Safety Stock, Reorder Recommendations, Scenario Parameters, Accuracy Metrics, Status, Audit Fields.

Validations: Forecast parameters validated, predicted demand numeric, scenarios tested, accuracy metrics computed.

Workflows: Forecast execution triggers inventory planning recommendations, alerts generated for stock-outs or overstock, integration with procurement and production modules, dashboards updated in real-time.

16.5 Order & Logistics Management

Forms / Screens: Sales Order Fulfillment, Purchase Order Receipt, Shipment Tracking, Delivery Scheduling, Carrier Management, Logistics Dashboard.

Fields: Order ID, Customer/Supplier, Item Details, Quantity, Warehouse, Shipping Method, Carrier, Tracking Number, Estimated Delivery Date, Status (Pending, In Transit, Delivered, Returned), Approval Status, Audit Fields.

Validations: Orders validated against stock availability, shipping details complete, delivery dates within SLA, approvals enforced for exceptions.

Workflows: Order creation triggers fulfillment workflow, shipment triggers notifications, delivery updates dashboards, integration with Finance for invoicing, integration with CRM for customer notifications.

16.6 Returns, Warranty & Reverse Logistics

Forms / Screens: Return Request, Return Approval, Warranty Claim, Reverse Logistics Tracking, Return Reports.

Fields: Return ID, Order ID, Customer ID, Item ID, Reason, Quantity, Status (Pending, Approved, Processed, Closed), Warranty Details, Refund/Replacement Action, Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Return eligibility validated, warranty coverage verified, quantity and item match order, approvals enforced.

Workflows: Return request triggers approval workflow, approved returns update stock automatically, integration with Finance for refunds, dashboards reflect reverse logistics metrics.

16.7 Reporting & Dashboards

Forms / Screens: Inventory Reports, Stock Valuation Reports, Procurement Reports, Supplier Performance Reports, Forecast Accuracy Reports, Order Fulfillment Reports, Logistics Dashboards.

Fields: Report ID, Name, Module, Filters (Item, Warehouse, Supplier, Date Range, Status), Data Fields (Stock Levels, Movements, Costs, Orders, Supplier Rating, Forecast Accuracy), Export Options, Schedule, Audit Fields.

Validations: Data accuracy verified, filters validated, scheduled reports triggered correctly, dashboards updated in real-time.

Workflows: Scheduled report generation triggers notifications, dashboards update automatically, alerts generated for anomalies or thresholds, integration with BI and AI modules for advanced analytics.

16.8 Integrations & Automations

Automations: Automatic stock replenishment recommendations, purchase order approvals, shipment notifications, stock adjustments triggers, demand forecasting updates inventory planning.

Integrations: ERP/Finance for cost and GL updates, CRM for sales order data, Projects for material allocation, AI for predictive demand forecasting, Logistics providers, BI for dashboards, HR for resource planning in warehouses.

Key Features: Complete inventory management, warehouse operations, procurement, supplier management, demand forecasting, order fulfillment, logistics tracking, returns & reverse logistics, reporting & analytics, automation and predictive features, cross-module integration, audit-ready and compliance integrated.
