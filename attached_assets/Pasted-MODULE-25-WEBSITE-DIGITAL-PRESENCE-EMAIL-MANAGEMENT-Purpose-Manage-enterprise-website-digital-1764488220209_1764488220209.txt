MODULE 25: WEBSITE, DIGITAL PRESENCE & EMAIL MANAGEMENT

Purpose: Manage enterprise website, digital content, marketing communications, and email campaigns to engage customers, generate leads, support sales, and integrate with CRM, Marketing, ERP, Projects, HR, Finance, BI, AI, and Compliance modules.

Important Note for Replit: Before creating any website, digital content, or email management objects, workflows, or dashboards, Replit must check for existing implementations. Do not duplicate or recreate anything already built. Only create missing elements or enhancements.

25.1 Website Content Management

Forms / Screens: Website Page Creation/Edit, Content Blocks, Templates, Media Library, SEO Configuration, Website Dashboard.

Fields: Page ID, Title, URL, Template, Content Type (Text, Image, Video, Form), Status (Draft, Published, Archived), Meta Tags, Keywords, Author, Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, URL uniqueness validated, SEO tags validated, media properly linked, approval enforced for publishing.

Workflows: Content submission triggers approval workflow, published pages update dashboards, integration with CRM and Marketing modules for lead capture, AI for content recommendations.

25.2 Digital Forms & Lead Capture

Forms / Screens: Form Builder, Form Fields Configuration, Form Submission Management, Lead Capture Dashboard.

Fields: Form ID, Name, Target Module (CRM, Marketing, Projects), Fields (Text, Number, Dropdown, File Upload), Validation Rules, Submission Status, Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, field types validated, duplicate submissions prevented, data mapped to correct module.

Workflows: Form submission triggers lead creation in CRM, notifications sent to responsible users, dashboards update automatically, integration with AI for lead scoring.

25.3 Email Campaign Management

Forms / Screens: Campaign Creation, Email Template Designer, Segmentation, Scheduling, Analytics Dashboard.

Fields: Campaign ID, Name, Type (Promotional, Transactional, Newsletter), Target Audience, Send Date/Time, Status, Email Content, Metrics (Open Rate, CTR, Bounce Rate), Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, email addresses validated, compliance rules (CAN-SPAM, GDPR) applied, approval required for high-impact campaigns.

Workflows: Campaign scheduling triggers notifications, emails sent automatically, responses logged in CRM, dashboards reflect performance metrics, integration with AI for personalization and predictive targeting.

25.4 SEO & Digital Marketing Tools

Forms / Screens: SEO Configuration, Keyword Management, URL Mapping, Analytics Integration, Dashboard.

Fields: Page ID, Keywords, Meta Description, Meta Tags, URL Slug, Status, Analytics Metrics, Audit Fields.

Validations: Keywords validated, URL structure correct, analytics tracking setup verified, mandatory SEO fields enforced.

Workflows: SEO updates reflected in dashboards, AI-driven recommendations suggest improvements, integration with BI for traffic and conversion analytics.

25.5 Analytics & Reporting

Forms / Screens: Website Traffic Reports, Lead Conversion Reports, Email Campaign Reports, Engagement Dashboard.

Fields: Report ID, Name, Module/Source, Metrics (Visitors, Leads, Clicks, Opens, Conversions), Filters (Date Range, Campaign, Page), Export Options, Schedule, Audit Fields.

Validations: Data accuracy validated, metrics calculated correctly, dashboards render accurately, scheduled reports executed.

Workflows: Reports generated on-demand or scheduled, dashboards update automatically, AI modules provide predictive insights, integration with CRM, BI, and Marketing modules.

25.6 Integrations & Automations

Automations: Automated lead capture from forms, campaign scheduling, email notifications, dashboard updates, AI-driven personalization, predictive content suggestions.

Integrations: CRM for lead management, Marketing for campaigns, ERP/Finance for sales data, Projects for campaign tracking, HR for content approval workflows, AI/BI for analytics, Compliance for regulatory checks.

Key Features: Website content management, digital forms and lead capture, email campaign creation and analytics, SEO management, dashboards and reporting, automated workflows, AI-powered personalization, cross-module integration, audit-ready and compliance integrated.
