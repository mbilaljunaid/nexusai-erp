MODULE 27: HUMAN RESOURCES (HR) & PAYROLL MANAGEMENT

27.1 Employee Master & Profile Management

Forms / Screens: Employee Creation/Edit, Personal Details, Job Details, Emergency Contacts, Employee Dashboard.

Fields: Employee ID, Name, DOB, Gender, Address, Contact Details, Department, Designation, Role, Reporting Manager, Date of Joining, Employment Type (Full-Time, Part-Time, Contract), Status (Active, On Leave, Resigned, Terminated), Skills, Qualifications, Bank Details, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, contact info validated, reporting hierarchy checked, duplicate employee prevention, data accuracy verified.

Workflows: Employee creation triggers onboarding checklist, notifications sent to managers and HR, dashboards reflect employee status, integration with Payroll, Projects, Finance, and Compliance modules.

27.2 Recruitment & Onboarding

Forms / Screens: Job Requisition, Candidate Management, Interview Scheduling, Offer Management, Onboarding Checklist.

Fields: Job ID, Title, Department, Hiring Manager, Candidate ID, Resume, Interview Date, Status (Applied, Shortlisted, Interviewed, Offered, Hired, Rejected), Offer Details, Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, duplicate candidates prevented, interview schedule conflicts checked, approvals enforced for offers.

Workflows: Job posting triggers notifications, candidate stage changes update dashboards, offer acceptance triggers onboarding process, integration with HR, Payroll, and Compliance modules.

27.3 Attendance & Leave Management

Forms / Screens: Attendance Entry, Leave Request, Leave Approval, Shift Scheduling, Attendance Dashboard.

Fields: Employee ID, Date, In-Time, Out-Time, Shift, Leave Type (Sick, Casual, Annual, Unpaid), Leave Balance, Status (Pending, Approved, Rejected), Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Attendance times validated, leave balance checked, overlapping leaves prevented, mandatory approvals enforced.

Workflows: Leave submission triggers approval workflow, attendance updates payroll calculations, dashboards reflect real-time employee presence, integration with Projects and Finance for resource allocation and cost tracking.

27.4 Performance & Appraisal Management

Forms / Screens: Goal Setting, Performance Review, Appraisal Input, Feedback, Performance Dashboard.

Fields: Employee ID, Goals, Target Metrics, Review Period, Reviewer, Rating, Comments, Status (Draft, Submitted, Approved), Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Goals validated, ratings within scale, reviewer assignment checked, approvals enforced.

Workflows: Goal assignment triggers notifications, review completion triggers appraisal workflows, dashboards reflect performance metrics, integration with Payroll for incentive calculation.

27.5 Training & Learning Management

Forms / Screens: Training Program Setup, Employee Enrollment, Training Progress, Feedback, Learning Dashboard.

Fields: Training ID, Program Name, Department, Employee List, Start/End Dates, Status, Completion %, Feedback Score, Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, enrollment validated, progress tracked, feedback collected, approvals enforced.

Workflows: Training assignment triggers notifications, completion updates dashboards, integration with HR and Performance modules for skill tracking.

27.6 Payroll & Compensation

Forms / Screens: Salary Structure, Payroll Processing, Payslip Generation, Tax & Deduction Management, Payroll Dashboard.

Fields: Employee ID, Basic Salary, Allowances, Deductions, Taxes, Net Pay, Payment Date, Status, Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Salary calculations validated, statutory compliance enforced, approvals for salary revisions, deductions verified.

Workflows: Payroll processing triggers notifications, payslips generated automatically, integration with Finance for payments, dashboards updated in real-time, compliance reports generated.

27.7 Benefits & Employee Self-Service

Forms / Screens: Benefits Enrollment, Claims Management, Self-Service Portal, HR Dashboard.

Fields: Employee ID, Benefit Type, Enrollment Period, Claims Details, Status (Pending, Approved, Rejected), Approval Workflow, Audit Fields.

Validations: Benefit eligibility validated, claims accuracy checked, approvals enforced.

Workflows: Enrollment or claims trigger notifications, updates reflected in dashboards, integration with Payroll and Finance, AI modules provide recommendations for benefit optimization.

27.8 Reporting & Analytics

Forms / Screens: HR Reports (Headcount, Attrition, Attendance, Leave, Training), Payroll Reports, Performance Reports, HR Dashboard.

Fields: Report ID, Name, Module, Filters (Department, Employee, Period, Status), Metrics (Salary, Attendance, Performance, Training Completion), Export Options, Schedule, Audit Fields.

Validations: Data accuracy validated, filters verified, scheduled execution tested, dashboards render correctly.

Workflows: Reports generated on-demand or scheduled, dashboards update automatically, integration with BI and AI for predictive HR analytics.

27.9 Integrations & Automations
