MODULE 12: COMPLIANCE & GOVERNANCE

Purpose: Ensure organizational adherence to statutory regulations, internal policies, audit requirements, risk management, and corporate governance across ERP, Finance, HR, CRM, Projects, Inventory, EPM, AI, and BI modules.

Important Note for Replit: Before creating any compliance or governance objects, workflows, or reports, Replit must check for existing implementations. Do not duplicate or recreate anything already built. Only create missing elements or enhancements.

12.1 Regulatory Compliance

Forms / Screens: Compliance Rule Configuration, Regulatory Reporting Setup, Compliance Audit Logs, Compliance Dashboard.

Fields: Compliance ID, Module/Process, Rule Name, Description, Jurisdiction, Effective Date, Expiry Date, Responsible Owner, Status (Active, Inactive), Risk Level, Audit Notes, Approval Status, Created By, Created Date, Modified By, Modified Date.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, rules mapped to correct modules/processes, deadlines validated, jurisdiction-specific rules applied, approval workflow enforced.

Workflows: New compliance rule triggers approval workflow, rule updates trigger notifications, compliance violations trigger alerts, compliance dashboards updated in real-time, audit logs maintained.

12.2 Risk Management

Forms / Screens: Risk Register, Risk Assessment, Risk Mitigation Plan, Risk Monitoring Dashboard, Risk Reports.

Fields: Risk ID, Module/Process, Risk Description, Risk Category, Likelihood, Impact, Risk Score, Mitigation Actions, Responsible Owner, Status (Open, Mitigated, Closed), Review Date, Approval Status, Audit Fields.

Validations: Risk scoring validated, mitigation plans mandatory for high-risk items, review dates enforced, responsible owners assigned.

Workflows: New risks trigger approval workflow, risk mitigation tasks trigger notifications, escalations for high-risk items, periodic risk review updates dashboards, integration with projects and finance modules for risk exposure tracking.

12.3 Audit & Internal Controls

Forms / Screens: Audit Plan, Audit Execution, Audit Findings, Control Tests, Corrective Action Tracking, Audit Reports.

Fields: Audit ID, Module/Process, Audit Type (Financial, Operational, IT, Compliance), Auditor, Findings, Severity, Recommendations, Status (Open, In Progress, Closed), Control Tests Performed, Corrective Actions, Audit Date, Approval Status, Audit Fields.

Validations: Audits scheduled per plan, findings validated, corrective actions assigned and tracked, audit completion status enforced.

Workflows: Audit triggers notifications to responsible parties, corrective action tasks trigger workflow, follow-up audits scheduled automatically, audit dashboards updated, compliance metrics monitored.

12.4 Policy Management

Forms / Screens: Policy Creation, Policy Approval, Policy Communication, Policy Acknowledgment Tracking, Policy Revision.

Fields: Policy ID, Policy Name, Description, Module/Process Affected, Effective Date, Expiry Date, Approval Status, Responsible Owner, Version, Acknowledgment Status, Audit Fields.

Validations: Policies approved before publication, version control enforced, acknowledgment tracking mandatory, mandatory policies communicated to relevant employees.

Workflows: Policy creation triggers approval workflow, distribution triggers acknowledgment requests, policy revisions trigger notifications, dashboards track compliance with policies.

12.5 Incident & Exception Management

Forms / Screens: Incident Reporting, Exception Logging, Incident Resolution, Escalation Rules, Incident Dashboard.

Fields: Incident ID, Module/Process, Incident Type, Description, Reported By, Date/Time, Severity, Status (Open, In Progress, Resolved, Closed), Root Cause, Corrective Action, Escalation Level, Approval Status, Audit Fields.

Validations: Mandatory fields enforced, severity and impact assessed, responsible owner assigned, resolution deadlines enforced.

Workflows: Incident submission triggers notification and assignment, escalations triggered automatically for overdue or high-severity incidents, resolution updates dashboards, integration with compliance and audit modules.

12.6 Reporting & Dashboards

Forms / Screens: Compliance Reports, Risk Reports, Audit Reports, Policy Compliance Dashboards, Incident Reports.

Fields: Report ID, Report Name, Module, Filters (Module, Process, Date Range, Risk Level, Status), Data Fields (Rule Violations, Risk Scores, Audit Findings, Corrective Actions, Incident Status, Policy Acknowledgments), Export Options (PDF, Excel, CSV), Scheduled Execution, Audit Fields.

Validations: Reports include all relevant compliance and governance data, filters validated, scheduled reports triggered correctly, access permissions enforced.

Workflows: Scheduled reports trigger automated notifications, dashboards update in real-time, alerts for non-compliance or exceptions, integration with BI tools for analytics.

12.7 Integrations & Automations

Automations: Automatic alerts for compliance violations, risk threshold breaches, audit follow-ups, incident escalation, policy acknowledgment reminders.

Integrations: Finance/ERP modules for financial compliance, HR for statutory compliance and policy enforcement, Projects for risk tracking, Inventory for stock compliance, BI/Analytics for dashboards, Audit Management Systems.

Key Features: Regulatory compliance, risk management, internal controls, policy management, incident tracking, audit-ready dashboards, automated alerts, cross-module integration, real-time compliance monitoring.
