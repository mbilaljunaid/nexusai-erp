1. Marketplace Overview

Build an App Marketplace where:

Third-party developers can build, publish, and sell apps/modules.

Tenants (customers) can browse, purchase, install, subscribe, upgrade, or uninstall apps.

Platform Admin controls governance, approvals, and revenue sharing.

Revenue sharing is configurable (default 0% platform fee, adjustable later).

The marketplace supports:

One-time purchases

Subscriptions (monthly / yearly)

Free apps

Trial versions

Open-source & commercial apps

2. Core Roles & Permissions
2.1 Platform Admin

Suspend apps or developers

View global transactions & revenue

Manage categories, tags, pricing models
